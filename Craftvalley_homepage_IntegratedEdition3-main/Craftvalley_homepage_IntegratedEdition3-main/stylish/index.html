構想 — Concept

What is Craftvalley?

Craftvalley is a regional initiative uniting master artisans across the mountainous corridor stretching from western Toyama Prefecture through Hida City in Gifu to Komatsu City in Ishikawa.

🏔️

Place

A living landscape of mountains, rivers and ancient trade routes

🤲

People

Multi-generational artisans who carry living traditions

✨

Craft

Objects made with patience, purpose and Japanese precision

The Craftvalley Region

The Craftvalley zone spans three prefectures along Japan's central mountain ranges — a ribbon of cultural heritage that few visitors have discovered.
